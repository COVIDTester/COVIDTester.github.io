COVID Tester

During this worldwide crisis, effective testing for COVID-19 has proven to be essential for efforts to mitigate the spread of the virus. To maximize the effectiveness of available testing it is important that information about known testing centers is made available. The free app COVID Tester is designed to do just that, simply opening the app on your phone reveals the locations of all testing centers in the vicinity, and provides important information about them. Getting tested can provide peace of mind for you and your family in some cases. While in other cases, it can save the lives of you and those around you. That is why it is our goal to make getting tested as easy as possible.
